# Privacy Policy for Bubble Pop

*Last updated: 21/11/2025*

Bubble Pop ("the App") is developed by a sole developer based in Oxford,
England ("we", "us", or "our"). We are committed to protecting your
privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how the App collects, uses, and
safeguards your information.

## 1. Information We Collect

### 1.1 Automatically Collected Data

The App does not collect any personally identifiable information (PII)
directly from users.

However, non-personal data may be collected automatically by third-party
services such as Google AdMob (e.g., device identifiers, ad
interactions, coarse location if enabled by Google).

## 2. Permissions Used

The App requires **Internet Access** to download new levels and display
ads through Google AdMob.

## 3. Advertising and Third-Party Services

The App displays ads using Google AdMob. Google may use cookies or
device identifiers to display personalized or non-personalized ads.

Google Privacy Policy: https://policies.google.com/privacy\
Google Partner Sites Data Use: https://policies.google.com/technologies/partner-sites

## 4. How We Use Information

We use network communication only to deliver gameplay content and
support ads. We do not sell or share personal information.

## 5. Children's Privacy

We do not knowingly collect personal data from children under 13. Google
AdMob may serve non-personalized ads for child-directed content.

## 6. Data Security

We take reasonable measures to secure data transmission but cannot
guarantee absolute security.

## 7. International Users

By using the App, you consent to the processing of non-personal data in
the UK and by third-party services globally.

## 8. Changes to This Privacy Policy

Updates will be posted within the App or on the Google Play Store.
